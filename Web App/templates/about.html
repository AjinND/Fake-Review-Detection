Fake Review Detector

The project Fake Review Detection is aimed to detect the fake reviews present in the e-commerce website, so as to help the customers to find the amount of fake and real reviews present. In most cases, reviews posted may be genuine, but there may be some exceptions where managers hire people to post fake reviews, either praise or demote the product, so as to increase their margin of profit. We aim at finding these type of reviews with the help of Machine Learning techniques. Fake Review Detector is a product that classifies the reviews present in the e-commerce site as "Real" or "Fake".

Classifier

The project uses Navie Bayes Classifier for the review classification. The classifier is a probabilistic classifier which classifies the data based on the probability. First step is to collect the data from the e-commerce website using a crawler. The it performs a sentimental analysis to check whether the reviews are of positive sentiment or not. It then classifies the reviews as fake or real.

The classifier is trained with a dataset containing 21000 datas. With these datas, the classifier gives an accuracy of about 80%.
